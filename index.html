Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

image:Sally Vegas

Fresh Cramberries Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegard
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fullyu absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tought I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
